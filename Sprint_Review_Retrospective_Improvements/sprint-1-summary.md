# Sprint 1 Summary

## Executive Summary
Sprint 1 delivered a usable room booking system that allows employees to reserve, filter, and cancel room bookings.
While advanced constraints were deferred, the sprint produced tangible user value and clear learning outcomes.

## Business Value Delivered
The system reduces administrative overhead, prevents overcapacity bookings, and improves meeting coordination within the organisation.

## Key Accomplishments
- Implemented basic room booking functionality
- Enabled room filtering by capacity
- Established booking cancellation flow

## Challenges Faced
- Overcommitment beyond historical velocity
- Complexity of booking conflict resolution logic
- External dependency on email notification services

## Metrics
- Planned story points: 24
- Completed story points: 11
- Actual velocity: 11

## Estimation Insights
Stories with unclear acceptance criteria consistently exceeded estimates.
Future sprints will break complex stories into smaller, testable increments.
